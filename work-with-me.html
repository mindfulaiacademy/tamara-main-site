Questions

Common questions

Do I need any experience with breathwork or meditation?

No. Most clients come with no prior breathwork experience. The sessions are structured and guided - you do not need to know anything beforehand. If you have tried meditation and found it difficult or frustrating, breathwork often works differently for precisely that reason - it works with the breath as an active mechanism, not a passive one.

Is this therapy?

No. This is regulation work - structured, somatic, and focused on nervous system function and performance. It is not therapy, and it is not a substitute for clinical mental health support. If you are currently working with a therapist or psychiatrist, breathwork and somatic regulation can complement that work. They are separate disciplines.

What does a session actually look like?

Sessions are conducted online and typically last 60 to 90 minutes. They combine guided breathwork, somatic awareness practices, and integration time. The 9D format uses immersive, multi-layered sound design that you experience through headphones - it is significantly different from standard guided meditation audio. No special equipment is required. A quiet space and a good pair of headphones is enough.

How quickly do results show up?

Many clients notice a tangible shift within the first session - a different quality of stillness, a change in how they feel under pressure, a sense of landing back in their body. Sustained change - in decision clarity, sleep quality, stress response, and leadership presence - builds over weeks of consistent practice. The six-week programme is structured specifically to create durable change, not a temporary state that fades when the session ends.

I don't know if I need the programme or 1:1 sessions - how do I decide?

Start with a discovery call. In 30 minutes, we can establish where you are, what you are looking for, and which format is the more appropriate fit. There is no obligation and no hard sell. If neither option is right for you at this moment, I will say so.

Not sure where to start?

A 30-minute discovery call is the most straightforward first step. We identify what you need and which path fits.
