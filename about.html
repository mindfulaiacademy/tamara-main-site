About

Tamara Korstick

Certified Oxygen Advantage Instructor · 9D Breathwork Facilitator · Based in Dubai since 2015

The story

I spent fifteen years in international corporate environments - working across markets, headquartered in Dubai since 2015. I understood performance, delivery, and the cost of sustained high output. I knew what it took to show up, deliver, and maintain the appearance that everything was under control.

I also understood, from the inside, what it looks like when the body starts keeping score.

The solution I found was not a wellness programme. It was nervous system regulation - structured, science-informed, and effective in ways that nothing else I had tried came close to. I trained as a Certified Oxygen Advantage Instructor and 9D Breathwork Facilitator. I built a practice. I began running sessions, retreats, and immersive experiences in Dubai.

I am now the bridge I needed to exist: someone who speaks boardroom and body fluently. Many practitioners have one or the other. I have both. That is the work - and that is the niche.
